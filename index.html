üîé The CEFR Framework#

The CEFR framework defines language proficiency in six levels: A1 (Beginner), A2 (Elementary), B1 (Intermediate), B2 (Upper-Intermediate), C1 (Advanced), and C2 (Proficiency). Each level builds on the skills and knowledge gained in the previous level. So, you’ll be able to progress at your own pace!
